# Checklist

## Labor

### Early
- [ ] Remove rugs or cover flooring around birth pool and bed
- [ ] Clear off surfaces in bedroom
- [ ] Remove bedrail
- [ ] Cover headboard
- [ ] Prepare bassinet (wait to install)
- [ ] Make bed (mattress protector, postpartum sheets, waterproof layer, throwaway sheets)
- [ ] Inflate tub and install liner
- [ ] Turn up water heater temperature; set reminder to reduce postpartum
- [ ] Make/offer snacks for MOM
- [ ] Make/offer electrolytes and water to MOM
- [ ] Remind MOM to use bathroom
- [ ] Gather placenta photo props
- [ ] Thaw casserole and sourdough
- [ ] Make [laboraide](TODO) or other electrolyte drink
- [ ] Offer for MOM to use tub (can use Epsom salt)

### Active Labor
- [ ] Call midwives
- [ ] Fill tub (99-101F)

### After Labor / Immediate Postpartum
- [ ] BABY checkout by medical staff
- [ ] MAMA checkout by medical staff
- [ ] Strip bedding (top sheets and waterproof layer; leave bottom sheets)
- [ ] Gather towels, clothes, and other soiled fabrics
- [ ] Start laundry
- [ ] Create space in bed for MOM and BABY
- [ ] Install bassinet
- [ ] Serve food and drinks or MOM
- [ ] Gather remaining birth supplies in plastic tub
- [ ] Drain and deflate birth tub

## Postpartum
> Anticipate needs; support MOM to support BABY. <br>
> BABY duties are opportunities to create a bond with BABY. <br>
> Create calm amidst the chaos to help MOM and BABY rest, recover, and grow.

### Goals

1. BABY feeding every 2-3 hours
1. BABY diaper output tracked and validated
1. MOM eating at least 3 meals and 2 snacks
1. MOM drinking at least 100 oz water (plus electrolytes)
1. DAD and KID(S) eating/drinking like normal
1. DAD and/or VISITORS completing chores
